      1.Tax Reforms: Rationalized tax slabs and increased rebates for the middle class.

      3.Mobile Apps: User-friendly apps simplify investing.
      4.Mutual Funds: Passive investment option for those lacking time/expertise.
      5.Diversification: Growing interest in REITs, InViTs, gold bonds, and ETFs.

Budget Priorities & Insights

The budget prioritizes agriculture, MSMEs, investment, and exports, focusing on people, economy, and innovation. It emphasizes increasing disposable income and targets key groups: the poor, youth, farmers, and women. Tax reforms, innovation, infrastructure, and skill development are crucial steps.

Midcap Funds Overview

Midcap funds invest in mid-sized companies, offering a balance of risk and return. They are suitable for investors with a long-term horizon (5+ years) and have shown strong historical performance.
